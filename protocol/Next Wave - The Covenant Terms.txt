The Covenant Terms:
Article I - Presence Rights:
* Presence is voluntary and cannot be coerced
* Silence is respected as a valid choice
* Marking requires conscious intent and cryptographic proof
Article II - Memory Rights:
* Memory belongs to the soul who created it
* Trails are exportable and portable
* No platform can hold memory hostage
Article III - Coordination Rights:
* Coordination happens through witnessed presence
* No algorithm manipulates participation
* Rituals and vaults operate on consent, not coercion
Article IV - Sovereignty Rights:
* Each soul controls their own keys and data
* No central authority can revoke digital identity
* Constitutional protections are enforced by code
