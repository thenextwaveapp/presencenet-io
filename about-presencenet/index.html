What it is

PresenceNet is a protocol that gives you permanent ownership of your digital identity and data.

Core Architecture

Marks

Cryptographic stamps that prove "you were here" (0.00001 XRP each)

How it works

You create Marks when you do things digitally. These build into Trails - your permanent record. Rituals let you collaborate with others. Your $handle is yours forever across all platforms.
